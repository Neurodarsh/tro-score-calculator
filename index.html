TBI-ROTEM Prognostic (TRP) Score

An integrated score for predicting outcome in Traumatic Brain Injury.

1. GCS on Admission
2. Rotterdam CT Score
3. Contusion Index
4. Admission Hemoglobin
5. ROTEM Profile (Use highest scoring abnormality)
